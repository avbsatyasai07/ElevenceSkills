Web scraping is the process of extracting data from websites using automated scripts or tools.

Popular Scraping Tools

  • Selenium: Great for dynamic content and browser automation.
  • Playwright: Fast, modern alternative with multi-browser support.
  • Scrapy: Powerful framework for large-scale scraping jobs.

Ethical Considerations

Always respect robots.txt, avoid overloading servers, and never scrape personal or sensitive data.

Compliance & Legality

Check site terms of service and ensure your scraping activities align with legal boundaries.

Implement rate limiting and retries to avoid getting blocked.
